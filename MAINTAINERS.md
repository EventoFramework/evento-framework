## Maintainer Duties

- Triage issues and pull requests.
- Keep CI, release automation, and dependency maintenance healthy.
- Review security-sensitive changes and coordinate vulnerability disclosure.
- Keep `CHANGELOG.md`, `SECURITY.md`, and release notes current.

## Becoming a Maintainer

Maintainer access is granted by existing maintainers based on sustained, high-quality contributions,
sound technical judgment, and demonstrated care for the project's users and compatibility promises.
